About Lumina

Lumina is a conceptual semantic search prototype designed to reveal the hidden connections across European cultural heritage. By utilizing structured metadata beyond traditional boundaries, users can explore how artistic and musical styles—from the Renaissance to Modernism—intertwine. This project was developed to bridge the gap between technical databases and humanistic cultural appreciation.
